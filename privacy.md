# TickMe Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 2025

## Data Collection
TickMe does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data.
All tasks, notes, and settings remain on your device.

## Permissions
TickMe may request access to:
- Notifications (task reminders)
- Calendar (event scheduling)
- Photos (profile picture)

These permissions are only used to enable app features.

## Third Parties
TickMe does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.
